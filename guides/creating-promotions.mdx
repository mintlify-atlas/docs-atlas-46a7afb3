The Marketing modules enable you to create promotional campaigns that drive sales and client loyalty. Beils Dashboard offers three types of promotions: Coupons (discount codes), Bonuses (multi-session packages), and Gift Cards.

## Types of Promotions

### Cupones (Coupons)
Discount codes that reduce purchase prices. Ideal for:
- Seasonal promotions
- First-time client discounts
- Loyalty rewards
- Limited-time offers

### Bonos (Bonuses)
Pre-paid multi-session packages. Perfect for:
- Session bundles (e.g., "Buy 5, Get 1 Free")
- Treatment courses
- Membership programs
- Advance bookings

### Giftcards (Gift Cards)
Purchasable vouchers with stored value. Great for:
- Holiday gifts
- Special occasions
- Client referral rewards
- Corporate gifting programs

## Creating Coupons

Access the coupon manager at **Marketing > Cupones**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Coupon Form">
    Click **"+ Nuevo Cupón"** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Coupon Code">
    Create a unique, memorable code:
    
    **Good Examples:**
    - `WELCOME20` - Clear purpose, includes value
    - `SUMMER25` - Seasonal, easy to communicate
    - `VIP15` - Targets specific customer segment
    
    **Avoid:**
    - Random codes like `X7K9PQ` (hard to remember)
    - Offensive or confusing terms
    - Too-long codes (keep under 15 characters)
    
    <Tip>
      Use uppercase for codes - they're easier to read and look more professional.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Discount Type">
    Choose how the discount applies:
    
    **Percentage Discount:**
    - Select "Porcentaje" (Percentage)
    - Enter value (e.g., 20 for 20% off)
    - Best for: Flexible discounts that scale with purchase size
    
    **Fixed Amount:**
    - Select "Cantidad Fija" (Fixed Amount)
    - Enter euro amount (e.g., 10 for €10 off)
    - Best for: Specific value promotions, easier to budget
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Discount Value">
    Enter the numerical value:
    - For percentage: 10, 15, 20, 25, etc.
    - For fixed amount: 5.00, 10.00, 20.00, etc.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Description">
    Write a clear description visible to staff:
    - What the promotion is for
    - Any special conditions
    - Internal notes
    
    Example: "Summer promotion for new clients - 20% off first visit"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Usage Limits">
    Control coupon availability:
    
    **Max Uses (Optional):**
    - Leave empty for unlimited uses
    - Set a number (e.g., 100) to cap total redemptions
    
    **Minimum Purchase (Optional):**
    - Require minimum spending to use coupon
    - Example: €50 minimum for €10 off coupon
    - Prevents loss-making small purchases
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Validity Period">
    **Valid Until (Optional):**
    - Leave empty for no expiration
    - Select end date for time-limited promotions
    - Expiration happens at end of the selected day
    
    <Tip>
      For seasonal campaigns, set clear end dates (e.g., "September 30"). For ongoing loyalty programs, leave validity open.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Status">
    Toggle **Activo** / **Inactivo**:
    - **Activo**: Coupon can be used immediately
    - **Inactivo**: Saved but not yet active (for future campaigns)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Coupon">
    Click **"Crear Cupón"** to activate the promotion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Coupon Strategy Examples

**New Client Welcome:**
```

Type: Percentage
Value: 15%
Min Purchase: €30
Max Uses: Unlimited
Valid Until: (empty - ongoing)
```

## Creating Bonuses (Multi-Session Packages)

Access bonus management at **Marketing > Bonos**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Bonus Form">
    Click **"+ Nuevo Bono"** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Name the Bonus">
    Create a clear, descriptive name:
    
    **Good Names:**
    - "10-Session Facial Package"
    - "Monthly Massage Membership"
    - "Quarterly Treatment Bundle"
    
    Include the number of sessions so clients immediately understand the offer.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Description">
    Explain what's included:
    - Service type covered
    - Validity period
    - Usage restrictions
    - Benefits vs. individual sessions
    
    Example: "Package of 10 facial treatments. Use within 6 months. Save €50 vs. individual pricing!"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Link to Service (Optional)">
    **Service Vinculado:**
    - Select a specific service if the bonus applies to one treatment type
    - Leave empty if valid for multiple services
    
    <Note>
      Linking a service helps with:
      - Automatic pricing calculations
      - Booking restrictions
      - Usage tracking
      
      Leave unlinked for flexible "credit" style bonuses.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Total Sessions">
    **Total Sessions:**
    Enter the number of sessions included in the package.
    
    Common values:
    - 5 sessions (starter package)
    - 10 sessions (popular choice)
    - 12 sessions (monthly for a year)
    - 20 sessions (intensive programs)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Package Price">
    **Price:**
    Enter the total price for all sessions.
    
    Pricing strategy:
    ```
    Individual Session Price: €60
    10 Sessions at Full Price: €600
    Package Price: €500 (€100 savings, 16.7% discount)
    Per-Session Cost: €50
    ```
    
    <Tip>
      Offer 15-20% discount vs. individual sessions to incentivize package purchases while maintaining profitability.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Status">
    Toggle **Activo** / **Inactivo**:
    - **Activo**: Available for purchase in TPV
    - **Inactivo**: Hidden but retained in system
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Bonus">
    Click **"Crear Bono"** to make it available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bonus Card Display

Once created, each bonus shows:
- Status badge (Activo/Inactivo)
- Bonus name
- "X Sesiones" count badge
- Description text
- Linked service (or "Válido para múltiples servicios")
- Total price
- Per-session calculated price in parentheses

## Managing Gift Cards

Access gift card management at **Marketing > Giftcards**.

<Note>
  Gift card functionality follows similar patterns to bonuses but focuses on monetary value rather than session counts. Configuration details depend on your system version.
</Note>

### Gift Card Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Effective Gift Card Programs:**
  
  1. **Standard Denominations**: Offer €25, €50, €100, €150 options
  2. **Clear Expiration**: 12 months is standard, 24 months for higher values
  3. **Custom Amounts**: Allow custom values for personalization
  4. **Attractive Packaging**: Physical cards increase perceived value
  5. **Easy Redemption**: Train all staff on redemption process
  6. **No Fees**: Avoid maintenance or inactivity fees
</Tip>

## Using Promotions in Practice

### Applying Coupons at Checkout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Coupon Validity">
    When a client provides a coupon code:
    1. Go to **Marketing > Cupones**
    2. Search for the code
    3. Verify:
       - Status is "Activo"
       - Not expired (check Valid Until date)
       - Under max uses limit
       - Cart total meets minimum purchase
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Calculate Discount">
    **For Percentage Coupons:**
    ```
    Cart Subtotal: €100
    Coupon: 20%
    Discount Amount: €100 × 0.20 = €20
    ```
    
    **For Fixed Amount Coupons:**
    ```
    Cart Subtotal: €75
    Coupon: €10
    Discount Amount: €10
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply in TPV">
    1. Open TPV and build cart normally
    2. In the "Descuento / Cupón" field, enter the calculated discount amount
    3. Total updates automatically
    4. Process payment
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Coupon Usage">
    After sale, the system automatically:
    - Increments "current_uses" counter
    - Disables coupon if max uses reached
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Selling Bonuses

<Steps>
  <Step title="Client Inquiry">
    When a client asks about package deals:
    1. Show them available bonuses in **Marketing > Bonos**
    2. Explain sessions included, total price, and savings
    3. Note any service restrictions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Process Purchase in TPV">
    1. Open TPV
    2. Select the client first (required for bonuses)
    3. Switch to **Bonos** tab
    4. Add the bonus to cart
    5. Process full payment
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm with Client">
    Explain:
    - "You now have 10 sessions available"
    - "Valid for 6 months from today"
    - "Book online or call us anytime"
    - "We'll track your remaining sessions"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Usage">
    As client books and completes sessions:
    - System decrements remaining sessions
    - View balance in client profile
    - Notify client when sessions are running low
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Redeeming Gift Cards

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Gift Card">
    When client presents gift card:
    1. Check **Marketing > Giftcards** for card code
    2. Verify:
       - Card is active
       - Not expired
       - Has remaining balance
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply Balance">
    In TPV:
    1. Build cart normally
    2. Apply gift card balance as discount
    3. If balance exceeds cart total, note remaining amount
    4. If cart exceeds balance, collect difference via normal payment
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Card Balance">
    After redemption:
    - Deduct used amount from gift card balance
    - Provide receipt showing remaining balance
    - Deactivate card if balance reaches zero
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Creating a Campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define Campaign Goal">
    Identify what you want to achieve:
    - Increase new client acquisition
    - Boost revenue in slow periods
    - Encourage advance bookings
    - Increase average transaction value
    - Promote specific services
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Promotion Type">
    Match type to goal:
    - **Coupons**: New client acquisition, seasonal sales
    - **Bonuses**: Client retention, revenue stability
    - **Gift Cards**: Gifting occasions, cash flow
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Strategic Pricing">
    Calculate financially sound discounts:
    ```
    Break-Even Analysis:
    Service Cost: €30 (product + labor)
    Regular Price: €60
    Margin: €30 (50%)
    
    Safe Discount Range: 15-25% (€51-45 final price)
    Minimum Discount Price: €35 (to maintain positive margin)
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Promotion">
    Build the offer in the appropriate module:
    - Use clear, action-oriented naming
    - Set appropriate limits (uses, dates, minimums)
    - Write compelling descriptions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Promote to Clients">
    Communicate the offer through:
    - Email campaigns
    - Social media posts
    - In-center posters
    - Staff training (so they mention it)
    - Website banners
    - SMS to client list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Performance">
    Monitor:
    - Redemption count (check "current_uses")
    - Revenue generated
    - New vs. returning client split
    - Average cart value change
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Optimize">
    After campaign:
    - Analyze what worked
    - Adjust discount levels for next time
    - Identify most popular offers
    - Refine targeting
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing and Managing Promotions

### Editing a Coupon

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Coupon">
    In **Marketing > Cupones**, find the coupon by searching or browsing.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open Edit Form">
    Click the **three-dot menu (⋮)** and select **Editar**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Modify Fields">
    Update any settings:
    - Extend expiration date
    - Increase max uses
    - Change discount value
    - Adjust minimum purchase
    - Update description
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **"Guardar"** to update the coupon.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Changing discount values on active coupons may confuse clients who already have the code. Consider:
  - Creating a new coupon code instead
  - Honoring the original terms for existing holders
  - Clear communication about changes
</Warning>

### Deactivating Promotions

To end a promotion without deleting:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit the Promotion">
    Open the promotion in edit mode.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Change Status">
    Toggle status to **Inactivo**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    The promotion remains in the system but can't be used.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Deactivating is better than deleting because:
  - Historical data is preserved
  - You can reactivate if needed
  - Reporting remains accurate
  - You can reference past campaigns
</Tip>

### Deleting Promotions

<Warning>
  Deletion is permanent. Only delete if:
  - Promotion was created in error
  - Never actually used
  - Duplicate of another promotion
  
  Otherwise, deactivate instead.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

### Financial Protection

<Tip>
  **Prevent Loss-Making Promotions:**
  
  1. **Set Minimum Purchase Requirements**: Especially for percentage discounts
  2. **Cap Maximum Discount**: Even on percentage coupons (e.g., max €50 off)
  3. **Limit Total Uses**: Prevents budget overruns
  4. **Set Clear Expiration**: Limits liability
  5. **Track Redemption Rates**: Watch for abuse patterns
  6. **One Coupon Per Transaction**: Prevent stacking unless intentional
</Tip>

### Client Communication

<Tip>
  **Clear Promotion Terms:**
  
  Always communicate:
  - Exact discount amount or percentage
  - Minimum purchase requirements
  - Expiration date
  - Applicable services (inclusions/exclusions)
  - Maximum uses per customer (if limited)
  - Non-transferable policy (if applicable)
  
  Example:
  "Get 20% off your first visit! Use code WELCOME20 at checkout. Valid on services €50 or more. Expires December 31, 2025. One use per customer."
</Tip>

### Staff Training

<Steps>
  <Step title="Regular Updates">
    Weekly team meetings should cover:
    - New promotions launched
    - Expiring promotions
    - Redemption procedures
    - Common questions and answers
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Reference Materials">
    Provide staff with:
    - Printed list of active coupons and terms
    - Step-by-step TPV discount application guide
    - Contact person for promotion questions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Empowerment">
    Allow staff to:
    - Proactively mention promotions
    - Apply manager-approved exceptions
    - Suggest new promotion ideas
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

### Coupon Not Appearing in System

Check:
1. Status is set to "Activo"
2. Valid Until date hasn't passed
3. Max Uses hasn't been reached
4. Search spelling is correct

### Client Says Discount Isn't Working

Verify:
1. Coupon code entered correctly (case-sensitive?)
2. Cart total meets minimum purchase
3. Coupon hasn't expired
4. Items in cart are eligible (not all products may qualify)
5. Coupon hasn't reached its use limit

### Bonus Purchase Not Associated with Client

Ensure:
1. Client was selected in TPV **before** adding bonus
2. Sale completed successfully (check sales history)
3. Correct client profile (not a duplicate)

If issue persists, contact system administrator to manually associate the bonus.

### Gift Card Balance Incorrect

Steps to resolve:
1. Check gift card transaction history
2. Verify all redemptions were recorded
3. Look for partial redemptions
4. Manual adjustment may be needed - document reason
